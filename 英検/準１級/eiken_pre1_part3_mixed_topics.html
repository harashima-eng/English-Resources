The Winning Formula (40-45 Sec)

Follow this structure for every question. Examiners expect a clear opinion, two supported reasons, and a conclusion.

1
Opinion — State your position clearly (5 sec)
2
Reason 1 + Example — First supporting point with concrete detail (15-20 sec)
3
Reason 2 + Example — Second supporting point with evidence (15-20 sec)
4
Conclusion — Restate your position with conviction (5 sec)

Filler Phrases — Buy Time

Silence is penalized. Use these phrases naturally to buy thinking time.

I suppose...〜だと思いますが…
Actually...実は…
In my opinion...私の意見では…
Personally speaking...個人的に言えば…
I would argue that...私はこう主張します…
Looking at it from another angle...別の角度から見ると…
Mixed A — 3 Questions
Q1 Should fast food be taxed more heavily?
Intermediate

Think About It First

  • Health problems caused by fast food?
  • How would tax help? (discourage consumption)
  • Where could tax money go? (healthcare)
  • Who would it hurt most? (low-income families)
  • Is it fair to control food choices?
  • Better alternatives? (education)
Key vocab: obesity discourage healthcare costs personal choice unfair burden
Yes

"Yes, fast food should be taxed more. It contributes to obesity and health problems. Moreover, the tax revenue could fund public healthcare. So this policy would improve public health overall."

Japanese

はい、ファストフードにはもっと課税すべきです。肥満と健康問題の原因となっています。さらに、税収は公的医療に資金を提供できます。だからこの政策は全体的に公衆衛生を改善するでしょう。

Director's Notes

  • "contributes to" 「〜の原因となる」。因果関係
  • "tax revenue" 「税収」。集めたお金
  • "fund public healthcare" 「公的医療に資金を提供する」
No

"I don't think fast food should be taxed more. This would unfairly burden low-income families who rely on cheap food. Furthermore, people should have the freedom to choose what they eat. So education about nutrition is more effective than taxation."

Japanese

ファストフードにもっと課税すべきではないと思います。これは安い食べ物に頼る低所得家庭に不当な負担をかけるでしょう。さらに、人々は何を食べるか選択する自由を持つべきです。だから栄養に関する教育の方が課税より効果的です。

Director's Notes

  • "unfairly burden" 「不当な負担をかける」
  • "rely on" 「〜に頼る」。依存する
  • "freedom to choose" 「選択する自由」
Q2 Is space exploration worth the cost?
Advanced

Consider Both Sides

  • Opinion: Is it worth the money? (YES)
  • Reason 1: Scientific discoveries benefit?
  • Reason 2: Technology spinoffs? (GPS, medical tech)
  • Conclusion: Long-term value?
Key vocab: scientific advancement technological innovation long-term benefits
Model Answer

"Yes, I believe space exploration is worth the cost. It drives scientific advancement and technological innovation. Moreover, many everyday technologies like GPS came from space programs. So the long-term benefits justify the investment."

Japanese

はい、宇宙探査はコストに見合う価値があると思います。科学の進歩と技術革新を推進します。さらに、GPSのような多くの日常技術は宇宙プログラムから生まれました。だから長期的な利益は投資を正当化します。

Director's Notes

  • "drives" 「推進する」。前進させる
  • "came from" 「〜から生まれた」。起源
  • "justify the investment" 「投資を正当化する」
Q3 Should animal testing for cosmetics be banned?
Intermediate

Consider Both Sides

  • Opinion: Should it be banned? (YES)
  • Reason 1: Animal welfare concerns?
  • Reason 2: Alternative methods available?
  • Conclusion: Cosmetics aren't essential?
Key vocab: cruel alternatives exist not essential
Model Answer

"Yes, animal testing for cosmetics should be banned. It's cruel and unnecessary since alternative testing methods exist. Moreover, cosmetics are not essential products like medicine. So there's no justification for animal suffering in this case."

Japanese

はい、化粧品の動物実験は禁止すべきです。代替テスト方法が存在するので、残酷で不必要です。さらに、化粧品は医薬品のような必須製品ではありません。だからこの場合、動物の苦しみを正当化する理由はありません。

Director's Notes

  • "cruel and unnecessary" 「残酷で不必要」。強い否定
  • "alternative methods" 「代替方法」。他の選択肢
  • "no justification for" 「〜の正当化はない」
Mixed B — 3 Questions
Q4 Should companies adopt a four-day work week?
Intermediate

Consider Both Sides

  • Opinion: Good idea? (YES)
  • Reason 1: Employee wellbeing?
  • Reason 2: Productivity? (studies show same output)
  • Conclusion: Win-win situation?
Key vocab: work-life balance productivity employee satisfaction
Model Answer

"Yes, I think a four-day work week is beneficial. It improves work-life balance and employee wellbeing. Furthermore, studies show that productivity doesn't decrease. So companies and employees both benefit from this arrangement."

Japanese

はい、週4日勤務は有益だと思います。ワークライフバランスと従業員の幸福を改善します。さらに、研究によると生産性は低下しません。だから企業と従業員の両方がこの取り決めから利益を得ます。

Director's Notes

  • "beneficial" 「有益な」。プラス効果
  • "employee wellbeing" 「従業員の幸福」
  • "doesn't decrease" 「低下しない」。変わらない
Q5 Should public transportation be free?
Basic

Think About It First

  • Environmental benefits? (less cars)
  • Accessibility? (help low-income people)
  • Who pays? (taxpayers)
  • Quality concerns? (overcrowding, maintenance)
Key vocab: reduce emissions accessible taxpayer burden quality
Yes

"Yes, public transportation should be free. It would reduce car usage and emissions. Additionally, it would help low-income people access jobs and services. So free transit benefits both the environment and society."

Japanese

はい、公共交通機関は無料であるべきです。自動車の使用と排出を減らすでしょう。さらに、低所得者が仕事やサービスにアクセスするのを助けるでしょう。だから無料の交通機関は環境と社会の両方に利益をもたらします。

Director's Notes

  • "reduce car usage" 「自動車の使用を減らす」
  • "emissions" 「排出物」。CO2など
  • "access jobs and services" 「仕事やサービスにアクセスする」
No

"I don't think public transportation should be free. Someone has to pay for maintenance and operations through taxes. Furthermore, free services often become overcrowded and poorly maintained. So a reasonable fare system is more sustainable."

Japanese

公共交通機関は無料であるべきではないと思います。誰かが税金で維持管理と運営費用を支払わなければなりません。さらに、無料サービスはしばしば混雑し、維持管理が悪くなります。だから妥当な運賃システムの方が持続可能です。

Director's Notes

  • "maintenance and operations" 「維持管理と運営」
  • "overcrowded" 「混雑した」。人が多すぎる
  • "sustainable" 「持続可能な」。長く続けられる
Q6 Do celebrities have too much influence on young people?
Intermediate

Consider Both Sides

  • Opinion: Too much influence? (YES)
  • Reason 1: What do young people imitate? (lifestyle, behavior)
  • Reason 2: Are celebrities good role models?
  • Conclusion: Need for balance?
Key vocab: role models impressionable unrealistic standards
Model Answer

"Yes, I think celebrities have too much influence. Young people are impressionable and often copy celebrity behavior. Moreover, celebrities promote unrealistic standards of beauty and success. So this influence can be harmful to youth development."

Japanese

はい、有名人は影響力が強すぎると思います。若者は影響を受けやすく、しばしば有名人の行動を真似します。さらに、有名人は美しさと成功の非現実的な基準を促進します。だからこの影響は若者の発達に有害である可能性があります。

Director's Notes

  • "impressionable" 「影響を受けやすい」。感受性が強い
  • "copy celebrity behavior" 「有名人の行動を真似する」
  • "unrealistic standards" 「非現実的な基準」
  • "harmful to youth development" 「若者の発達に有害」
Mixed C — 2 Questions
Q7 Should students be required to wear school uniforms?
Basic

Consider Both Sides

  • Equality? (no fashion competition)
  • Focus? (less distraction)
  • Self-expression? (individuality)
  • Cost? (expensive for families)
Key vocab: equality focus on learning self-expression restrict individuality
Yes

"Yes, school uniforms should be required. They promote equality by eliminating fashion competition. Furthermore, uniforms help students focus on learning rather than appearance. So uniforms create a better learning environment."

Japanese

はい、学校の制服は必須であるべきです。ファッション競争をなくすことで平等を促進します。さらに、制服は学生が外見よりも学習に集中するのを助けます。だから制服はより良い学習環境を作ります。

Director's Notes

  • "promote equality" 「平等を促進する」
  • "eliminating fashion competition" 「ファッション競争をなくす」
  • "focus on learning" 「学習に集中する」
No

"I don't think uniforms should be required. Students should express their individuality through clothing. Moreover, uniforms can be expensive for families. So allowing casual clothes gives students more freedom and reduces costs."

Japanese

制服は必須であるべきではないと思います。学生は服を通して個性を表現すべきです。さらに、制服は家族にとって高価になることがあります。だから私服を許可することで学生により多くの自由を与え、コストを削減します。

Director's Notes

  • "express their individuality" 「個性を表現する」
  • "expensive for families" 「家族にとって高価」
  • "reduces costs" 「コストを削減する」
Q8 Should there be an age limit for social media use?
Advanced

Consider Both Sides

  • Opinion: Need age limit? (YES)
  • Reason 1: Risks for young children? (cyberbullying, inappropriate content)
  • Reason 2: Mental health impact?
  • Conclusion: Protection necessary?
Key vocab: protect children cyberbullying mental health inappropriate content
Model Answer

"Yes, there should be an age limit for social media. Young children are vulnerable to cyberbullying and inappropriate content. Additionally, social media can negatively affect mental health development. So age restrictions are necessary to protect children."

Japanese

はい、ソーシャルメディアには年齢制限があるべきです。幼い子供たちはサイバーいじめや不適切なコンテンツに対して脆弱です。さらに、ソーシャルメディアは精神的健康の発達に悪影響を与える可能性があります。だから子供を保護するために年齢制限が必要です。

Director's Notes

  • "vulnerable to" 「〜に対して脆弱」。影響を受けやすい
  • "cyberbullying" 「サイバーいじめ」。オンラインでのいじめ
  • "inappropriate content" 「不適切なコンテンツ」
  • "negatively affect" 「悪影響を与える」
